Flare-plot: Bring your networks to life

From the activity of proteins inside our body to airline traffic across the world, hundreds of systems are modeled as static networks. In reality though, these networks dynamically change over time. How do we visualize and make sense of dynamic networks? Flare-plots empower you to visualize and analyze dynamic networks.

Shown below is a network of molecular interactions for 30000 frames from an MD simulation of the pain- and addiction-related mu-opioid receptor. Click a label on the circle and the structure on the right will show the highlighted interactions.
